About Us


We are a group of computer science students who are passionate about creating innovative solutions. Our team consists of Hanna Abebe, Hanna Thomas, and Jumua Brhan. Together, we embarked on a mission to develop a platform for our college ACT, and we proudly named it EVALRater. The name EVALRater signifies our goal of providing evaluation and rating services for employee performance.

Although some modules are still in progress, EVALRater is designed to benefit both the college and its staff. We have included provisions for future work, ensuring that EVALRater has the potential to grow and evolve over time. Our dedication to excellence and our commitment to creating a valuable tool for evaluating and rating employee performance drive us forward. We are excited to contribute to the success of our college and its staff through EVALRater.

Mission

Our
Vision

Our
Values

Our values are at the core of our system. Through effective goal-setting, feedback, evaluation, recognition, and development opportunities, we strive to create an environment where everyone can thrive and contribute to our collective success.
